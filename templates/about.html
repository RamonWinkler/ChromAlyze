About Health Risk Report

Welcome to ChromAlyze, your personalized health risk analysis platform! Our mission is to empower individuals with insights about their coronary heart disease (CHD) risks, using state-of-the-art data analysis and machine learning techniques.

Our application provides a user-friendly interface to input health data and generates a comprehensive report based on scientifically validated models. Whether you're a healthcare professional or an individual curious about your heart health, ChromAlyze offers a reliable and easy-to-understand risk assessment.

How It Works

  • Input your health metrics in the form provided.
  • Our system analyzes the data using advanced machine learning algorithms.
  • Receive a detailed risk score along with actionable recommendations.

At ChromAlyze, we believe in making healthcare accessible, informative, and proactive. Join us in taking control of your heart health today.
